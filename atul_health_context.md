## 4. Supplements — exact protocol

### Nurokind-OD (B12)
- **Dose:** 1 tablet (1500 mcg methylcobalamin)
- **Frequency:** Daily
- **Timing:** 8:00 AM, with/after breakfast
- **How:** Sublingual — place under tongue 1–2 min, then swallow. No water for 10 min after.
- **Duration:** 12 weeks initially, then retest, then maintenance 2–3x/week for life (vegetarian)
- **Safety:** Water-soluble, impossible to overdose. OTC in India. ~₹150 for 10 tablets.

### Uprise-D3 (60,000 IU weekly sachet variant)
- **Dose:** 1 sachet (60,000 IU)
- **Frequency:** Once a week — Sunday
- **Timing:** With Sunday lunch (1:30 PM)
- **How:** Empty sachet into 30 ml toned milk, stir, drink at end of meal. Must be with fat-containing food — paneer sabzi at Sunday lunch is required for absorption.
- **Duration:** 8–12 week loading, then maintenance (~1000–2000 IU daily)

### Chia seeds (daily habit — already doing)
- **Dose:** 1 tbsp (15g) soaked overnight in 250 ml water
- **Timing:** 6:30 AM on waking (90 min before Nurokind-OD)
- **Why:** Fiber, omega-3 ALA, satiety, bowel regularity, passive B12 (minor)

## 6. Core principles (non-negotiable)

1. **Protein at every meal.** If a meal has no paneer, dal, curd, whey, soya, or peanuts — add one.
2. **Portion control beats food choice.** Halve rotis and rice from before. Double sabzi and dal.
3. **NEAT beats gym.** Walking, standing, fidgeting burns more weekly than workouts.
4. **Consistency over intensity.** 80% for 12 weeks beats 100% for 2 weeks then quitting.
5. **Miss a day, not a week.** The next meal is the reset button.
6. **Oil is measured, never eyeballed.** 5 ml (1 level teaspoon) per dish maximum. Cook must use a dedicated teaspoon.
7. **Post-workout shake is non-negotiable** on training days — 60g whey (2 scoops) + 250 ml toned milk within 60 min.

### Acidity rules (personal)

- No coffee on empty stomach
- Dinner by 8:30 PM, finish 3 hours before sleep
- No lying down after eating — sit or walk
- Stop at 80% full
- If acidity hits: 250 ml cold toned milk OR 100g plain curd
- Plan avoids besan at breakfast

**Equipment available:** Dumbbells (adjustable), walking treadmill, standing desk, yoga mat.

### Weekly schedule

| Day | Session | Duration | Time |
|---|---|---|---|
| Monday | Strength A (full body) | 40 min | 6:00 PM |
| Tuesday | Treadmill walk (split AM/PM) | 40 min | 7 AM + 9 PM |
| Wednesday | Strength B (full body) | 40 min | 6:00 PM |
| Thursday | Treadmill walk (split) | 40 min | 7 AM + 9 PM |
| Friday | Strength A (repeat) | 40 min | 6:00 PM |
| Saturday | Long cardio (intervals) | 50 min | Morning |
| Sunday | Rest (optional 20 min walk) | — | — |

### Starting weights

Pick dumbbells where **the last 2 reps of 12 feel hard but doable with clean form**. For 40-year-old beginner: usually **5–7 kg per dumbbell**. Progress every 2 weeks by 1–2 kg if 12 reps feel easy.

**Important until thyroid confirmed:** Only progress weight if no palpitations, chest tightness, or excessive fatigue from current weights.

### Strength A (Mon + Fri)

Warm-up: 5 min treadmill at 4.5 km/h.

1. **Goblet Squat** — hold 1 dumbbell at chest, squat down with back straight. 3 × 12, rest 60s
2. **Dumbbell Floor Press** — lie on mat, press dumbbells from chest until arms straight. 3 × 10, rest 60s
3. **One-Arm Dumbbell Row** — one knee on bed/chair, pull dumbbell to hip. 3 × 10 each side, rest 60s
4. **Romanian Deadlift** — hold dumbbells in front, hinge at hips, slight knee bend. 3 × 10, rest 60s
5. **Plank Hold** — elbows under shoulders, body straight. 3 × 30 sec, rest 45s

Cool-down: 10 min treadmill walk at 5 km/h.

### Strength B (Wed)

Warm-up: 5 min treadmill at 4.5 km/h.

1. **Dumbbell Deadlift** — stand up pushing through heels. 3 × 10, rest 60s
2. **Dumbbell Shoulder Press** — standing, press dumbbells from shoulder to overhead. 3 × 10, rest 60s
3. **Reverse Lunge** — dumbbells at sides, step back, knee to ground. 3 × 10 each leg, rest 60s
4. **Bent-Over Row** — hinge forward, back flat, pull both dumbbells to ribs. 3 × 10, rest 60s
5. **Dead Bug** — lie on back, extend opposite arm/leg slowly. 3 × 10 each side, rest 45s

Cool-down: 10 min treadmill walk at 5 km/h.

### Cardio days (Tue + Thu)

- Morning (7 AM): 20 min at 5 km/h
- Evening (9 PM, post-dinner): 20 min at 5 km/h
- Goal: 10,000 steps/day total

### Saturday long cardio (50 min intervals)

- Warm-up: 5 min at 4.5 km/h
- Intervals: 2 min at 6 km/h + 2 min at 4.5 km/h, repeat 10 times (40 min)
- Cool-down: 5 min at 4 km/h

### Pre and post workout

- **Before lifting (45–60 min):** 150 ml black coffee + 1 apple OR 1 banana
- **After lifting (within 60 min):** 60g whey (2 scoops) + 250 ml toned milk — non-negotiable

### Standing desk strategy

- Daily target: 3–4 hours standing during 8-hour workday
- Stand during: calls, email/Slack, code reviews, reading docs (high energy mornings)
- Sit during: deep focus coding, architecture, typing-heavy work
- Alternate every 45–60 min

### Walking treadmill strategy

- Use during: phone/video calls, Slack/email triage, reading PRs, tutorials, thinking sessions
- Avoid during: deep focus coding, heavy typing, detailed debugging
- Target: 3,000–5,000 steps from work-hours treadmill alone
- Combined with morning + evening walks = 10,000+ easily

### Micro-movement rules

- Every 90 min: stand up, 10 bodyweight squats, shoulder rolls
- After every meal: 10 min slow walk (kills post-meal glucose spike)
- Morning first thing: 20 min treadmill
- After dinner: 15 min walk

## 13. Tracking (iPhone + Apple Watch)

### Apps to use

- **Apple Health** (pre-installed) — central dashboard, pin Steps, Weight, Active Energy, Protein, Sleep, Resting Heart Rate
- **Apple Fitness** — set rings: Move 500 kcal, Exercise 45 min, Stand 12 hours
- **HealthifyMe** — Indian food database, log first 3–4 days to calibrate portions only
- **Water Minder** or **Waterllama** — 8 reminders matching water schedule

### Apple Watch setup

- Watch face: Modular or Infograph with Activity, Heart rate, Weather complications
- Settings → Activity → Stand Reminders ON
- Settings → Sleep: bedtime 10:45 PM, wake 6:30 AM
- Every strength session: Watch → Workout → "Traditional Strength Training"
- Every treadmill walk: "Indoor Walk"

### Metrics to track

| Metric | Frequency | Where |
|---|---|---|
| Weight | Sunday 7 AM (after toilet, before eating) | Apple Health |
| Steps | Automatic | Apple Watch |
| Resting heart rate | Daily (thyroid proxy) | Apple Health |
| Protein grams | First 3–4 days, then intermittent | HealthifyMe |
| Sleep duration | Automatic | Watch → Sleep |
| Workouts done | Every session | Watch Workout app |
| Waist | Monthly | Tape measure, log in Notes |
| Supplements taken | Daily tick | Reminders or Notes |

### Apple Watch RHR rule (thyroid monitoring)

- **Normal:** 60–75 BPM
- **Elevated:** 76–85 BPM (monitor)
- **Concerning:** consistently >85 BPM (flag to endocrinologist — possible hyperthyroid indicator)

### Don't track

- Exact calories daily (causes obsession)
- Daily weight fluctuations (lie)
- Macros beyond protein

## 14. Symptoms watchlist

### Hyperthyroid signs to monitor weekly

- Muscle weakness on stairs/lifting (currently present)
- Reduced sleep need (currently present)
- Racing heart, palpitations at rest
- Hand tremor (hold paper flat test)
- Heat intolerance
- Anxiety, irritability
- Hair thinning
- Neck swelling (goiter)
- Eye bulging or gritty feeling → urgent

### B12 deficiency signs to monitor

- Burning feet (currently present — should improve in 4–6 weeks on Nurokind)
- Tingling/pins and needles
- Numbness (if new → urgent)
- Balance problems (one-leg 20-sec stand test)
- Sore red tongue
- Brain fog, memory issues
- Shortness of breath on exertion

### Red flags — see doctor same day

- Chest pain or tightness during/after exercise
- Sustained palpitations >100 BPM at rest
- Sudden severe weakness, fainting, dizziness
- Eye changes (vision blur, bulging, severe dryness)
- Numbness spreading upward from feet
- Breathing difficulty disproportionate to effort

## 17. App requirements (what to build)

A single-page web app, **pure HTML + CSS + vanilla JavaScript**. No frameworks. No build step. Deploy to Vercel. Installable on iPhone as home screen web app.

### Must have

1. **Bottom nav with 5 tabs:** Meals, Exercise, Tips, Shopping, Tracker
2. **Meals tab:**
   - Day selector (Mon–Sun) with auto-select today's day
   - Day header showing training/cardio/rest badge
   - Macro bar showing protein, fat, carbs, calories that auto-update based on swaps
   - Meal cards (7 meals per day for most days) each showing items
   - **Item-level swap:** Each ingredient row has its own "Swap" button. Tap → bottom sheet opens with 3–5 alternatives (each showing their P/F). Tap alternative → item swaps, macros recalculate, rest of meal stays.
   - Swapped items visually marked (blue color, "swapped" tag)
   - Supplement items flagged with purple "Supplement" pill
   - Swaps persist in localStorage
3. **Exercise tab:**
   - Weekly schedule table
   - Cards for Strength A, Strength B, Cardio day, Saturday long cardio
   - Each exercise row with name, description, sets × reps, rest time
   - Tap-to-complete checkboxes per exercise (persists for today)
   - Pre/post workout rules highlighted
4. **Tips tab:**
   - Categorized cards: Core principles, Supplements, Acidity rules, Red flags, Standing desk, Walking treadmill, Micro-movements
   - Color-coded boxes (green = rules, yellow = tips, red = warnings, purple = medical)
5. **Shopping tab:**
   - Weekly list grouped by category (Dairy, Pulses, Protein, Grains, Nuts, Veggies, Fruits, Supplements)
   - Tap items to check off (persists), strikethrough when done
   - "Reset list" and "Check all" buttons
6. **Tracker tab:**
   - Weight log (add entry, see history)
   - Daily RHR input
   - Supplement taken today toggles (Nurokind, Chia, Uprise-D on Sundays)
   - Symptoms quick checklist for Sunday review
   - Workout completion summary for the week

### iOS specifics

- `apple-mobile-web-app-capable` meta tags for fullscreen home screen
- Custom SVG icon baked into HTML
- Safe area insets (notch, home indicator)
- No zoom on input focus (`user-scalable=no`)
- Touch-optimized (44px minimum tap targets)
- Bottom sheet modal style for swap picker

### Design notes

- Color palette: Primary blue `#1a4d7a`, protein red `#e74c3c`, fat amber `#f39c12`, green `#27ae60`, purple `#8e44ad`
- Card-based UI with rounded corners (12–14px)
- Font: system (-apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont)
- Background: `#f7f9fb` (soft gray)
- Minimal, clean, not flashy
