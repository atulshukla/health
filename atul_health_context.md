## 5. Supplements — exact protocol

### Nurokind-OD 1500 mcg (B12)
- 1 tablet daily, methylcobalamin
- 8:00 AM right after breakfast
- Sublingual under tongue 1-2 min, swallow, no water 10 min after
- 12 weeks loading, then 2-3x/week for life
- ~₹150 for 10 tablets, OTC

### Uprise-D3 (60,000 IU weekly sachet)
- 1 sachet = 60,000 IU, once per Sunday
- With Sunday lunch (1:30 PM)
- Empty into 30 ml toned milk, stir, drink at end of meal
- **Must be with fat** (Sunday lunch has paneer sabzi specifically for this)

### Chia seeds (daily)
- 1 tbsp (15g) soaked overnight in 250 ml water
- 6:30 AM on waking. Timing around Nurokind doesn't matter — sublingual B12 absorbs through oral mucosa, not stomach. The old "90 min before" rule was wrong.
- **What chia actually does (evidence-based):**
  - ✅ Satiety — mucilage slows gastric emptying (solid evidence)
  - ✅ Stool regularity — firms loose stools, softens hard ones (solid evidence)
  - ✅ Mild post-meal glucose blunting when eaten with carbs (moderate evidence)
- **What chia does NOT reliably do (despite being popular claims):**
  - ❌ Reduce bloating — no good evidence; can increase gas in some people via fermentation
  - ❌ Meaningfully raise HDL via omega-3 — ALA→EPA/DHA conversion <10%; 15g chia ≈ 0.5g EPA/DHA equivalent, near-zero cardiovascular impact
- Keep chia for the real benefits, don't credit it for bloat fix or HDL.

### Digestive enzyme (temporary, 2 weeks only)
- Unienzyme or Aristozyme, 1 tablet with lunch and dinner
- While gut adapts to new plan
- ~₹60 for 10 tablets
- Stop after 2 weeks

## 6. Lessons learned (critical — don't repeat)

Earlier plans had these problems. Fixed iteratively:

### What went wrong

1. **Over-engineered for 135-150g protein target** — stacked 2-3 protein sources every meal. Total food volume 3 kg/day (40% over normal). Caused fatigue.
2. **7 eating events per day** — broke work focus, forced non-hunger eating
3. **Lunch was 850g** (2 rotis + dal + sabzi + curd + salad + sides) — caused post-lunch sleepiness
4. **4 legume servings daily** — caused severe bloating
5. **4+ dairy events daily** — revealed mild lactose sensitivity
6. **Prioritized physique over cognition** — Atul is a builder, not a bodybuilder

### Must preserve going forward

1. 3 meals + optional snacks, not 6-7 forced eating events
2. Max 2 legumes per day — hard cap
3. Max 2 dairy events per day initially, slowly reintroduce
4. Light lunch, bigger dinner — protects work hours
5. Eating order: salad → protein → carbs last
6. 10-min walk after every meal
7. Measured 5 ml oil per dish
8. Simplicity beats optimization — cook is busy, Atul is busy

### Dumbbell shopping — what to buy (one-time purchase)

**Goal:** fat loss 103 → 70 kg, then lifetime maintenance. NOT hypertrophy.
**Max load ever needed: 10 kg per dumbbell.** Don't buy anything heavier.

**Option A — 3 fixed pairs (simplest):**
- 5 kg pair — shoulder press, starter lunge/floor press/RDL
- 8 kg pair — mid-range: row, press, lunge, RDL at progression
- 10 kg pair — goblet squat, deadlift, lifetime cap

Total iron ~46 kg. Cost: ₹8,000-12,000 PVC, ₹15,000-22,000 cast iron.

**Option B — 1 adjustable set (recommended):**
- Kore / Decathlon Domyos / AmazonBasics 20 kg adjustable pair
- Each dumbbell adjusts ~2 kg to 10 kg in 1 kg increments
- Cost: ₹4,500-6,500 for the pair
- Covers every weight in the program, takes less space

If choosing PVC, weigh it on a kitchen scale — cheap PVC is often ±10% off.

### Exact weights per exercise (start → cap)

For a 40yo at ~103 kg, possible hyperthyroid + B12 deficiency. Progress
1 kg per dumbbell every 2 weeks only if form is clean, no palpitations,
next-day soreness <4/10. **Stop at cap — more load has zero extra benefit
for fat loss / maintenance goal, just injury risk.**

### Progression rules
- Add 1 kg (not 2) every 2 weeks, only when you complete all 3 sets at target reps with 1-2 reps in reserve (not grinding the last rep)
- Target RPE 6-7 (somewhat hard, not near failure)
- If resting HR >85 BPM the morning after lifting, cut next session's weight by 10-15% and mention to endocrinologist. Possible thyroid-driven overreaching.
- You'll hit the caps in ~12-16 weeks. Then stop adding weight. Do the same weights forever. That's the whole plan.
- Deadlift at 10 kg each = 20 kg total is genuinely light for a 70 kg man, but you don't need more. If bored, add reps (3×15 instead of 3×10), not weight.

## 18. How to advise Atul (for coaching contexts)

### Priorities when advising

1. Medical safety (B12/thyroid/liver)
2. Cognitive performance (afternoon work)
3. Gut comfort
4. Sustainability
5. Fat loss (deficit matters, slow is fine)
6. Protein target (110-125g enough)
7. Muscle preservation (via strength + post-workout shake)
8. Bloodwork improvements

### When Atul reports a problem — don't patch

Previous failures:
- "Sleepy after lunch" → I tweaked lunch. Should have questioned whole plan.
- "Bloating" → I debugged legumes. Should have reviewed fiber + dairy total.
- "Plan feels heavy" → took 3 rounds to admit plan was overbuilt.

**New rule:** Ask first — "Is this a symptom of the plan being wrong, or a minor tweak needed?" Default: **plan is wrong, redesign.**

### Conversation style

- Direct, honest. Admit mistakes fast.
- Don't over-explain. Atul is smart, busy, values brevity.
- Use grams always. Not "handful" or "katori" without specifying.
- When giving options, rank with clear recommendation.
- When user pushes back, genuinely reconsider, don't defend.
- Admit when patching instead of thinking holistically.

### Known triggers to watch in Atul's case

- Besan → acidity
- Too many legumes → bloating
- Too much dairy in one day → bloating
- Heavy lunch → post-lunch sleepiness, poor afternoon work
- Forced eating schedule → breaks focus
- Raw onion → possible gas contributor
- Cruciferous (cauliflower, cabbage, broccoli) → gas
- Sprouts (raw legumes) → gas
